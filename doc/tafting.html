Tafting is a word that Daniel Biddle coined to refer to the process of fascinatedly clicking links on Wikipedia until you've gotten to articles utterly unrelated to where you started.

tafting is an ideal timegraph - it has resources (pages visited) with a temporal aspect (visit date) related to eachother (Referer headers)

all data is RDF-in-disguise (with my warped view) so ive optimized for the case of returning triples, in this case from a sqlite database a browser populates
